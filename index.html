How it works

Three things.
One practice.

Stop improvising. Build class flows from a library of over 350 exercises — structured by section, timed to the minute, and saved for whenever you need them again.

  • Warm up, main sequence, and cool down — organised the way you actually teach
  • 350+ exercises searchable by apparatus, body region, and level
  • Save your best flows and reuse them in one tap
  • Export beautiful PDF class plans to share with clients or studios

The debrief that used to live only in your head — now captured, structured, and working for you. Rate each session and build a teaching record that compounds over time.

  • Rate energy, flow, timing, engagement, and confidence after every class
  • Your Vibe Score — one honest, composite number for how the session landed
  • Leave notes for your future self between classes
  • A permanent record of your growth as an instructor

Most instructors improve by feel. Alora makes growth visible — your Teaching Profile radar, Vibe Score trends, and streak data paint a picture of the instructor you’re becoming.

  • A Teaching Profile radar that updates after every reflection
  • Vibe Score trends over weeks, months, and your entire career
  • Streaks and consistency data that hold you accountable
  • Insights that surface exactly where to focus next
