One number.
Total honesty.

Not a vanity metric. A mirror. Track it long enough and you’ll see something most instructors never get to see: the actual shape of your growth.

Muscle Focus

This is what no
other app shows you.
What you’re actually training.

“Most instructors don’t know what they’re actually training. They think they do — but the data tells a different story.”

Alora’s Muscle Focus maps every exercise across every class. It reveals the unconscious patterns in your programming — the muscles you’re overloading, the ones you’re ignoring, and the blind spots your clients will eventually feel.

  • See what you’re overtraining before it becomes a problem
  • Identify the blind spots in your programming
  • Refine your class design with data you can actually see
  • Track muscle balance across your whole schedule over time
